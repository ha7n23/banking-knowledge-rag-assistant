# Card Disputes Knowledge Base

## Duplicate Card Charges

Customers can report duplicate card transactions where the same merchant payment is charged more than once. These cases are reviewed using card transaction logs, merchant records, and settlement information.

## Failed Card Transactions

If a card transaction fails but the customer account is debited, the customer may raise a dispute. The bank checks transaction status, merchant records, and settlement information before deciding whether reversal or escalation is required.

## Card Safety

Customers should immediately report lost or stolen cards. The bank may block the card and issue a replacement according to the customer request and bank process.
